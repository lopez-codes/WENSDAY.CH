# User Guide - wensday.ch

Complete user guide for the wensday.ch business AI platform.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Create Your Account
1. Visit [wensday.ch](https://wensday.ch)
2. Click "Anmelden" (Sign In)
3. Choose your registration method:
   - Email and password
   - Google account
   - Swiss ID (planned)

### First Steps
1. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add company information
   - Select your industry
   - Set business goals

2. **Choose Your Plan**
   - Start with Free (10 messages/day)
   - Upgrade to Ultra, Pro, or wensday-core as needed

3. **Start Your First Chat**
   - Click "Neues Gespräch"
   - Ask your first business question
   - Experience Swiss-optimized AI responses

## 💬 Using the Chat Interface

### Starting a Conversation
```
1. Click "Neues Gespräch" in the sidebar
2. Enter your business question or request
3. Press Enter or click Send
4. Wait for the AI response with quality indicators
```

### Chat Features
- **Message History**: All conversations are saved automatically
- **Quality Scores**: See confidence levels for AI responses
- **Provider Selection**: Choose your preferred AI model (paid plans)
- **Export Options**: Download conversations as PDF or text

### Quality Indicators
- 🟢 **High Confidence (80%+)**: Highly reliable response
- 🟡 **Medium Confidence (60-79%)**: Good response, consider verification
- 🔴 **Low Confidence (<60%)**: Review carefully, may need human verification
- ✅ **Verified**: Manually verified as accurate
- 🔍 **Fact Checked**: Automatically cross-referenced

### Choosing the Right Provider
```
For Business Analysis: Claude or GPT-4
For Quick Questions: Gemini
For Research: Perplexity
For Current Events: Grok
For Creative Tasks: GPT-4
For Cost Efficiency: Gemini
```

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Message limit reached"
**Solution**: Upgrade your plan or wait until tomorrow for limit reset
**Prevention**: Monitor usage in your dashboard

#### "AI provider unavailable"
**Solution**: Switch to another provider or try again later
**Prevention**: Enable multiple providers (Ultra+ plans)

#### "Low confidence response"
**Solution**: Rephrase your question or ask for clarification
**Prevention**: Be specific and provide context

#### "Conversation not loading"
**Solution**: Refresh the page or clear browser cache
**Prevention**: Use latest browser version
